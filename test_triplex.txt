Project Overview
================

The Research and Development Knowledge Graph project aims to create a comprehensive knowledge base for managing research information.

Key Information:
- The project lead is Dr. Sarah Johnson from Imperial College London
- The project started in January 2024 and will run until December 2025
- The main deliverable is a detailed work package (WP) that includes collaboration with partners
- The next meeting is scheduled for the 3rd Monday of each month at 10-11 CET
- Any discrepancies apart from the ones discovered in KoM notes should be reported
- The homework assignment is to create a Detailed Work Plan with Involved Partners till Month 18
- The United Kingdom (UK) is the primary location for the project
- The project uses advanced NLP techniques for knowledge extraction

Important Dates:
- Kick-off Meeting (KoM): March 15, 2024
- First Review: September 2024
- Final Review: December 2025

Partners:
- Imperial College London (ICL) - Lead partner
- University of Cambridge - Technical partner
- University of Oxford - Research partner
